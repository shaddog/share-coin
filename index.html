Sharecoin

Sharecoin is a digital currency that operates on smart-contracts and follow the principles of the decentralization. Anyone can get $SHARE as reward for inviting other people into the  decentralized finances.

Guaranteed exchange $SHARE to $TON

All $TON coins sent to Invitation contracts are collecting in the ✰ TON Treasury ContractThe TON Treasury Contract is a smart-contract that acts as a repository for $TON in the system.

You always can exchange your $SHARE to $TON with a following rate: [Total issued $SHARE] / [Total collected $TON]

Where are we now

Reward size per invited person depends on the amount of people joined SHARE.
Halving happens when SHARE emission reaches a certain amount.

The next halving: 2^(12+n) SHARE, where n = number of halving
